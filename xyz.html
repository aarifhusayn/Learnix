Study & Work in Germany
Via Ausbildung

A specialized dual vocational training program that allows you to earn a monthly stipend while learning high-demand technical skills in German companies.

Earn While Learning

Receive a monthly stipend (approx. €800 - €1,200) from the company throughout the 3-year program.

Job Guarantee

95% transition rate into full-time permanent jobs with high salaries after completion.

Pathway to PR

Direct pathway to Permanent Residency (PR) in Germany after working for a few years post-Ausbildung.

Top Fields for Diploma Holders

German companies value the practical foundation of Diploma students.

IT Specialist

Software Development, Systems, & Networking.

Electronics

Mechatronics

Car Mechanic

What do you
Need to Start?

German Language Proficiency

Minimum B1 or B2 level certification (Goethe/Telc) is mandatory as the training is in German.

Academic Qualifications

Class 12th or a 3-year Diploma. Your certificates must be translated into German.

Age Limit

Generally between 18 to 28 years. Some companies accept older candidates for technical roles.

Required Documents

  • Updated CV (German Format)
  • Motivation Letter (Anschreiben)
  • Language Certificate (B1/B2)
  • Degree/Diploma Transcript

The Application Process

01

Learn German

Complete A1 to B1/B2 level courses from a certified institute.

02

Find Employer

Apply to companies in Germany via portals like Ausbildung.de or Make it in Germany.

03

Interview

Clear online interviews with the company and sign the contract.

04

Visa & Flight

Apply for Ausbildung Visa at the German embassy and start your journey.
