Lab Tasks

  • 1 Move the compass around the magnet to observe field direction.
  • 2 Observe magnetic field lines inside the bar magnet.
  • 3 Toggle "Planet Earth" mode to see real-world compass behavior.

Pro Tip

The compass needle's red end always points towards the Magnetic North. Change magnet strength to see how field intensity varies!
